Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

aconnectivity.com is committed to the privacy principles declared in our Manifesto. Visitors are not data sources — they are participants whose dignity and autonomy are non-negotiable. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we do not collect, and how we protect everyone who visits this site.

What We Collect

This site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We collect the absolute minimum:

  • Contact form submissions — name, organization, email, and message — submitted voluntarily by you
  • Basic server logs maintained by GitHub Pages (IP address, browser type, pages visited) — we do not control or access these directly

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies of any kind
  • We do not use tracking pixels or behavioral analytics
  • We do not use advertising networks or retargeting
  • We do not sell, share, or trade any visitor data
  • We do not build profiles of visitors

Contact Form Data

Information submitted via the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not stored in any third-party database, not shared with any party, and not used for marketing purposes. You may request deletion of your submission at any time.

Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect limited data as part of this service. We use no other third-party services, scripts, or analytics platforms.

Your Rights

  • The right to know what data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of any data you submitted
  • The right to disconnect from this platform at any time without penalty

Security

This site implements security headers on every page to protect visitors from cross-site scripting, clickjacking, and unauthorized data collection by third parties. Unauthorized scraping, crawling beyond normal indexing, or any attempt to interfere with this platform is prohibited.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy may be submitted via our contact page.
